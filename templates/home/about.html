Our Story

O-Learn was founded by group of Techies in 2021 with a vision of providing life-transforming learning experiences to learners around the world. Today, O-Learn is a global online learning platform that offers anyone, anywhere, access to online courses and degrees from leading universities and companies. O-Learn received B Corp certification in February 2021, which means that we have a legal duty not only to our shareholders, but to also make a positive impact on society more broadly, as we continue our efforts to reduce barriers to world-class education for all.

82 million learners, 100+ Fortune 500 companies, and more than 6,000 campuses, businesses, and governments come to O-Learn to access world-class learning—anytime, anywhere.



Our Mission

Making E-Learning better for everyone.

We help people achieve independence by making it easier to start, run, and grow a business. We believe the future of commerce has more voices, not fewer, so we’re reducing the barriers to business ownership to make commerce better for everyone.
